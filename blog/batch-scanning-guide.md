**Batch scanning** is a game-changer for anyone dealing with multi-page documents. Whether you're digitizing a contract, archiving receipts, or processing forms, ScanDocPro's batch mode saves hours of work.

## What is Batch Scanning?

Batch scanning allows you to:

- Scan multiple pages in one session
- Automatically organize pages in order
- Export as a single PDF document
- Process 50+ pages in seconds

## When to Use Batch Scanning

### Perfect For:

- **Multi-page contracts** and agreements
- **Expense reports** with multiple receipts
- **Research papers** and academic documents
- **Medical records** and insurance forms
- **Tax documents** and financial statements

## How to Batch Scan in ScanDocPro

### Step 1: Enable Batch Mode

Open the camera and tap the **Batch** icon. This enables continuous scanning.

### Step 2: Scan Pages

Capture each page:

- The app automatically detects page edges
- Review each scan before proceeding
- Reorder pages by dragging thumbnails

### Step 3: Enhance and Edit

Apply enhancements to all pages or individually:

- **Auto-enhance**: Fix lighting and contrast
- **Color modes**: Color, grayscale, or black & white
- **Crop**: Adjust borders per page

### Step 4: Export

Choose your export format:

- **PDF**: Standard document format
- **PDF/A**: Archival quality
- **Images**: Individual page images
- **Cloud**: Direct to Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.

## Pro Tips for Batch Scanning

### 1. Prepare Your Documents

- Remove staples and paper clips
- Align pages neatly
- Ensure good lighting
- Use a contrasting background

### 2. Optimize Settings

- Enable **Auto-capture** for faster scanning
- Set **Default enhancement** preferences
- Choose appropriate **File size** vs. quality balance

### 3. Stay Organized

- Use **Document naming** templates
- Add **Tags** for easy searching
- Create **Folders** by project or date

## Troubleshooting

### Pages Out of Order

Use the thumbnail view to drag and reorder pages before exporting.

### Blurry Scans

Ensure adequate lighting and hold the camera steady. Enable **Stabilization** in settings.

### File Too Large

Adjust the **Quality** slider or choose **Smart Compression** for optimal file size.
